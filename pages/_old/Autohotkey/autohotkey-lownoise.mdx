```bash filename="lownoise-no-mouse" copy
Tab::ENTER
LShift::LButton
LWin::MButton
LAlt::Del
Right::LButton
Capslock::RButton
LCtrl::Mbutton
Left::RButton
LCTRL & WheelUp::
Send, {Ctrl down}{c down}
Sleep, 5                                ;gives the PC time to process.
Send, {c up}{Ctrl up}
LCTRL & WheelDOWN::
Send, {Ctrl down}{V down}
Sleep, 5                                ;gives the PC time to process.
Send, {V up}{Ctrl up}
^WheelDown::Click
```
